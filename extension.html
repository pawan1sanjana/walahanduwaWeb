Our Factory Extension Services

At our tea factory, we believe that empowering farmers is the key to producing the highest quality tea. Our extension services focus on providing **training, technical support, and essential resources** to enhance productivity and sustainability. We work closely with farmers to ensure best farming practices, fair trade opportunities, and environmentally friendly tea production. Through these initiatives, we create a **strong and lasting partnership with our farming community**, fostering growth and innovation in the tea industry.

Our Services for Farmers

Farmer Training

Workshops and training sessions to enhance tea cultivation skills.

Fertilizer & Supply Support

Providing high-quality fertilizers and necessary agricultural inputs.

Quality Assessment

Ensuring the highest quality of green leaves before processing.

Fair Price Purchasing

Offering competitive prices for farmers' tea leaves to ensure fair trade.

Technical Support

Guidance on best farming practices and pest control methods.
