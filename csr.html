Our Corporate social responsibility

At Walahanduwa Tea Factory, we are committed to sustainability, ethical business practices, and community well-being. Our CSR initiatives aim to support the environment, local communities, and our valued farmers, employees.

Our CSR for the Community

Environmental Sustainability

We implement eco-friendly solutions to minimize our impact on the planet.

  • Adopting renewable energy sources such as solar power.
  • Reducing waste through responsible disposal and recycling.
  • Implementing water conservation and pollution control measures.

    • Community Welfare

    We actively support the well-being of local communities around our factory.

  • Providing educational support and infrastructure to local schools.
  • Improving healthcare facilities and conducting medical camps.
  • Developing clean water and sanitation projects.

    • Employee Well-being

    Our employees are the backbone of our success, and we ensure they thrive.

  • Ensuring fair wages and safe working environments.
  • Providing skill development and training programs.
  • Supporting worker housing and essential needs.

    • Sustainable Agriculture

    We encourage sustainable farming techniques to preserve our ecosystem.

  • Promoting organic and sustainable farming practices.
  • Conserving biodiversity and protecting native plant species.
  • Encouraging smallholder farmers to adopt eco-friendly cultivation methods.
