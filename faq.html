Frequently Asked Questions

Walahanduwa Tea Factory was established over 50 years ago and has a rich history of producing some of the finest tea in Sri Lanka. We prioritize quality and sustainability in every step of our production process.

We follow strict quality control measures throughout the entire production process, from selecting the best tea leaves to ensuring proper processing and packaging. Regular quality checks ensure that our tea meets high standards.
